WAREHOUSE OWNER ERP SCENARIOS - END-TO-END PLAYBOOK

Purpose
This file lists unique, real-world warehouse-owner scenarios you should solve in ERP,
with practical actions, monitoring, and success metrics.

============================================================
1) SAME ITEM, DIFFERENT SUPPLIER LABELS
============================================================
Risk:
- Duplicate stock records, reorder confusion, wrong pricing.

ERP approach:
- One master item code policy.
- Supplier part-number mapping to master item.
- Barcode aliases for supplier variations.
- Duplicate-blocking validation rules.

Operational checks:
- Daily duplicate item exception report.
- Daily unmapped supplier SKU report.

Success metric:
- Zero duplicate active SKUs.

============================================================
2) FAST-MOVING ITEM STOCKOUT BEFORE REORDER TRIGGERS
============================================================
Risk:
- Lost sales and emergency purchasing.

ERP approach:
- Dynamic reorder points based on 4-week velocity.
- Include supplier lead time + safety stock.
- ABC classification for reorder sensitivity.

Operational checks:
- Daily stock cover (days) dashboard by class.

Success metric:
- Fill rate > 98% for A items.

============================================================
3) SLOW-MOVING STOCK TYING UP CASH
============================================================
Risk:
- Dead inventory, markdowns, write-offs.

ERP approach:
- Aging buckets: 30/60/90/180+ days.
- Non-moving alerts with item owner assignment.
- Liquidation and substitution workflow.

Operational checks:
- Weekly non-moving inventory review.

Success metric:
- Inventory turns improve quarter over quarter.

============================================================
4) RECEIVING MISMATCH: PO VS RECEIPT VS INVOICE
============================================================
Risk:
- Incorrect on-hand stock and payment errors.

ERP approach:
- 3-way match controls.
- Over/under receipt tolerance settings.
- Exception hold reasons and approvals.

Operational checks:
- Daily GRN mismatch queue.

Success metric:
- Invoice mismatch rate < 1%.

============================================================
5) QUALITY HOLD DURING INBOUND
============================================================
Risk:
- Defective goods become sellable stock.

ERP approach:
- Quarantine location on receipt.
- Quality inspection checklist.
- Release/reject gate before availability.

Operational checks:
- Daily quarantine aging report.

Success metric:
- Zero rejected lot picked for shipment.

============================================================
6) EXPIRY CONTROL AND PRODUCT RECALL
============================================================
Risk:
- Compliance and customer safety issues.

ERP approach:
- Batch and expiry tracking.
- FEFO picking for expiring products.
- Lot genealogy and shipment traceability.

Operational checks:
- Daily expiry-in-30-days report.

Success metric:
- Full recall trace in under 2 hours.

============================================================
7) PICK/PACK ERRORS DURING PEAK VOLUME
============================================================
Risk:
- Returns, reship costs, customer complaints.

ERP approach:
- Wave picking by zone.
- Scan verification at pick and pack.
- Carton and content verification.

Operational checks:
- Shift-level pick accuracy dashboard by picker.

Success metric:
- Picking accuracy > 99.5%.

============================================================
8) PARTIAL SHIPMENT WITH CUSTOMER PRIORITY RULES
============================================================
Risk:
- SLA misses on high-value customers.

ERP approach:
- Allocation engine by priority tier + promised date.
- Margin-aware fulfillment prioritization.
- Backorder automation.

Operational checks:
- Hourly backorder allocation queue.

Success metric:
- On-time-in-full improves by priority class.

============================================================
9) RETURNS SURGE AFTER PROMOTION
============================================================
Risk:
- Warehouse congestion and value loss.

ERP approach:
- RMA reason codes.
- Disposition paths: restock/refurbish/scrap.
- Automatic credit-note path.

Operational checks:
- Daily return reason Pareto chart.

Success metric:
- Reduced return cycle time and improved recovery value.

============================================================
10) CROSS-WAREHOUSE STOCK IMBALANCE
============================================================
Risk:
- One site overstocked while another is out of stock.

ERP approach:
- Inter-warehouse transfer rules.
- Lead-time-aware transfer suggestions.
- Min/max policy by location.

Operational checks:
- Daily network imbalance report.

Success metric:
- Fewer emergency transfer requests month over month.

============================================================
11) SHRINKAGE AND UNEXPLAINED LOSS
============================================================
Risk:
- Margin erosion and possible theft/process failure.

ERP approach:
- Cycle counting by ABC class.
- Mandatory variance reason codes.
- Approval workflow for high variances.

Operational checks:
- Daily variance exceptions by location and handler.

Success metric:
- Shrinkage below target threshold.

============================================================
12) LABOR BOTTLENECKS AND OVERTIME SPIKES
============================================================
Risk:
- High operating costs and delayed dispatch.

ERP approach:
- Labor standards per process step.
- Forecast-to-shift staffing plan.
- Productivity scorecards.

Operational checks:
- Shift heatmap: throughput vs labor hours.

Success metric:
- Lower cost per order line and overtime percentage.

============================================================
13) DOCK CONGESTION AND CARRIER DELAYS
============================================================
Risk:
- Missed pickups, detention fees, dispatch delays.

ERP approach:
- Dock appointment scheduling.
- Load readiness checkpoints.
- Carrier performance tracking.

Operational checks:
- Hourly dock queue board.

Success metric:
- Reduced average truck dwell time.

============================================================
14) WAREHOUSE SYSTEM OR NETWORK OUTAGE
============================================================
Risk:
- Fulfillment operations stop.

ERP approach:
- Offline scan/picking mode.
- Printable emergency picking packets.
- Deferred sync and conflict resolution log.

Operational checks:
- Post-recovery sync conflict audit.

Success metric:
- Recovery time objective consistently met.

============================================================
15) FINANCE NEEDS REAL CASH TIED IN INVENTORY
============================================================
Risk:
- Poor cash-flow decisions due to low visibility.

ERP approach:
- Real-time inventory valuation dashboard.
- Aging-value view for excess and obsolete stock.
- Carrying-cost trend monitoring.

Operational checks:
- Weekly working capital review.

Success metric:
- Better service-level and cash balance over time.

============================================================
90-DAY EXECUTION PLAN
============================================================
Days 1-30:
- Clean item master data.
- Enforce SKU/barcode uniqueness.
- Launch receiving mismatch exceptions.

Days 31-60:
- Implement batch/expiry with FEFO.
- Run structured cycle count program.
- Activate customer-priority allocation rules.

Days 61-90:
- Add labor planning and dock scheduling.
- Finalize owner-level working-capital dashboard.
- Institutionalize weekly exception review cadence.
